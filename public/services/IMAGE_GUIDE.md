## Image Specifications

- **Format**: JPG (JPEG)
- **Dimensions**: 800x400 pixels (2:1 aspect ratio)
- **File Size**: Maximum 500KB per image
- **Quality**: High-resolution, professional images
- **Color**: Should match the site's professional, luxury aesthetic
- **Content**: Clear, relevant imagery for each service

## Image Guidelines

1. **Professional Quality**: Use high-quality images that reflect the premium nature of Frenaphok's services
2. **Consistency**: Maintain similar lighting, color temperature, and composition across all images
3. **Relevance**: Each image should clearly represent its corresponding service
4. **Optimization**: Compress images to ensure fast page load times
5. **Branding**: Consider incorporating amber/gold accents (matching the brand colors)
